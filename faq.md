## What types of businesses benefit most from using Dialzara?

Dialzara is designed for small businesses that receive a high volume of inbound calls and are at risk of missing calls due to limited staff or no receptionist. Business types that benefit include service providers, agencies, clinics, contractors, and any organization that needs consistent, always-on call handling. ## What happens if the AI agent cannot answer a question or gets stuck?

When the AI agent cannot answer a question, you can configure instructions for fallback behavior. For example, the agent can tell the caller it does not know the answer and indicate that a specialist will follow up. You can also enable live call transfers for callers who request human assistance.  [oai_citation:2‡Dialzara](https://dialzara.com/faqs?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
